PSN library import

Paste your npsso cookie to import your PS5/PS4 library automatically.

How to find your NPSSO token
  1. Open my.playstation.com in a browser and sign in.
  2. Press F12 to open DevTools, then open the Application tab.
  3. Under Storage, then Cookies, find npsso.
  4. Copy the 64-character value and paste it below.

The token is sent only to your local companion server (127.0.0.1), never to a third party.
